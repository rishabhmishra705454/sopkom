2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

Cookies may be:

  • Session cookies — temporary cookies that are erased when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies — cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them manually.
  • First-party cookies — set directly by the website you are visiting.
  • Third-party cookies — set by a domain other than the website you are visiting (e.g., analytics or font providers).

4. Third-Party Cookies and Services

Our Site uses third-party services that may place cookies or make network requests from your browser. These include:

  • Google Fonts — We use Google Fonts to serve the "Plus Jakarta Sans" typeface. When your browser loads the font, a request is made to Google's servers (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com), which may log your IP address and browser information. Google's privacy policy governs this data. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — The Tailwind CSS library is loaded from a content delivery network (CDN). The CDN provider may log standard server access data such as IP address and request timestamp.

Sopkom does not control the cookies set by these third-party services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of each provider for more information.

5.1 Browser Settings

You can manage or delete cookies directly through your browser settings. Instructions for common browsers:

5.2 Do Not Track

Some browsers support a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not wish to be tracked. As there is currently no universally accepted standard for DNT, our Site does not respond to DNT signals. However, you can use the cookie management options above to limit tracking.
